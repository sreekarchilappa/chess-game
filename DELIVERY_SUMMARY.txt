HOW TO USE RIGHT NOW
================================================================================

1. START THE GAME:
   cd /Users/Kiran/Applications/ChessApp
   python3 -m http.server 3000

2. PLAY:
   Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser

3. GAME FLOW:
   - Click "Play vs Bot" and select difficulty
   - Or click "2-Player Local" to play with a friend
   - Click pieces to select, click destination to move
   - Win 5 games at each difficulty to unlock the next level
   - View your stats in the main menu
